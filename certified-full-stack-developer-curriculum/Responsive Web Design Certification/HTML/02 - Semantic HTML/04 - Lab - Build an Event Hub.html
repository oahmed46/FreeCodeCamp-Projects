Dat Monday Meltdown

Welcome to London's premier Smash Ultimate local: DAT Monday Meltdown! Whether you're a serious grinder or a casual player, the vibes and atmosphere are undefeated and everyone will have plenty of time to socialise and play the game, in and out of the bracket. Despite the 64 player cap the bar is still open to the public so spectators are welcome with the condition that you purchase from the bar.

Albion Arena

After a 7 year hiatus, the Albion tournament series is back in business, with a new location, new events and new attractions for all of those in attendance!

Albion Arena opens the show for the Norwich Games Festival, one of the biggest public events Norwich has to offer, held across the first two days of the 8 day event!
