Hot Chocolate

Guide on how to make a nice, tasty, hot cocoa

Ingredients

  • 250ml milk (of your choice)
  • 1 tbsp cocoa
  • 1-2 tbsp soft light brown sugar
  • 25g dark or plain chocolate (finely chopped, plus a grating to serve)
  • 1 tbsp whipped cream (or squirty cream)

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk, cocoa, sugar and chocolate in a small pan over a medium heat until steaming and the chocolate has melted.
  2. Whisk to dissolve the cocoa.
  3. Pour into a mug, then add the cream to float on top.
  4. Grate over a little more chocolate to serve.
